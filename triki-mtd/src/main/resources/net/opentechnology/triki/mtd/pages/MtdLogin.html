Let's get started! In order to use opentax to manage you HMRC VAT returns, we require a two-stage login.

  1. Login as yourself using an existing online identity. This is quicker, more secure and more convenient than setting up separate login with us.
  2. Login to HMRC to authorise us to access your accounts. If you change your mind later, this permission can easily be revoked on the HMRC portal.

That's it, you can manager your HMRC VAT records.
